Academy of Wellbeing means a journey towards learning to make wellbeing real for the most marginalised

The Academy of Wellbeing(AoW), supported by YuWaah and anchored by Swasti, empowers young individuals to become advocates for Sexual Reproductive Health and Rights. With faculty, Coaches, and peers who are experts in Policy, Advocacy, and Communication, AoW, guides and equips the next generation of policy advocates.
